My name is Julia Rietveld and I am a front end developer with huge pasion for Wordpress, Search Engine optimisation and Web Accessibility. I am employed at a wonderful web agency but plan to find more balance in life by getting back to freelance work.

I am a big fan of separating the markup (HTML) from the styles (CSS) and the behavour (JS). In my own projects I prefer to use Sass mixins for the layout to keep my HTML as semantic as possible. My super power is CSS, I prefer Scss and if possible use this language above jQuery

High coding standards, well indented and commented code and good front end architecture and files organisation are very important voor me.
